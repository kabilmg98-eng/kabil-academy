Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

At Kabil Academy®, we prioritize clarity and parent alignment. This policy details refund parameters regarding diagnostic assessments, monthly subscription fees, and STEM hardware kits.

1. Diagnostic Assessment Fees

Initial diagnostic assessments and learn-style reports involve 1-on-1 scheduling with cognitive coaches. The fees for these diagnostic reports are non-refundable once the test session is initiated.

2. Monthly Subscription Cycles

Enrollment fees operate on standard monthly cycles. If a parent requests withdrawal from a batch within the first 7 days of the billing period, a prorated refund will be calculated and returned. No refunds are available for requests made after the first 7 days of the cycle.

3. STEM Hardware & Dance Kits

Fees paid for individual robotics kits, Arduino component boxes, sports uniforms, and dance fitness resources are non-refundable once the physical materials have been unpacked and distributed to the student.

4. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are processed via bank transfer to the parent's verified account parameters within 7-10 business days of official approval.
